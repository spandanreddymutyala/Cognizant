Community Bulletin

The city council is proud to announce a full calendar of community events for the summer season. Residents are encouraged to participate in activities ranging from arts and culture to fitness and wellness. All events are family-friendly and open to everyone regardless of age or background.

Volunteers are needed to help manage registrations and guide attendees at each venue. If you'd like to contribute, please visit the registration desk or contact the events team at cityevents@local.gov.in. Your involvement makes these events possible and meaningful for the whole community.

Special provisions have been made for senior citizens and differently-abled residents, including accessible seating, transport assistance, and dedicated help desks at every venue. We are committed to ensuring every resident can enjoy and participate fully in community life.

Stay updated by subscribing to our monthly newsletter. Get early bird access to event registrations, exclusive discounts, and behind-the-scenes stories from last year's most-loved events. Together, we build a stronger, more connected community.
